This folder “accent” contains data on word accents.

Public Contents as of 29 August 2025

    narada_accent_verb_20250829.xlsx: Data on the accent patterns of conjugated verb forms.

    Directory "wav": Audio data of the accents of conjugated verb forms.

    list_of_morphemes_20250829.xlsx: A list of morphemes referenced in the above files.

    奈良田方言の表記_20250414.xlsx: A list of phonetic symbols used for transcription of the Narada dialect.
